Goals

The chief goal of this project is to give the information of CossiePlay especially to youngsters. Additionally, to sell the customised costumes as well as second hand costumes to the customers (especially youngsters) is the sole purpose of this site.

Success Evaluation

This site is crafted keeping in mind the chief goal of luring the youngsters to the site ultimately turinng them into customers who buy costumes. The success of this site depends on how many youngsters become the customers of CossiePlay. People of all age groups can visit the site or become customers but ideally this project will be successful if more number of customers are youngsters.

Target Audience

The target audience of this project is the youngsters. This site is trying to lure the youngsters to visit the sites and in turn make them customers. The site is very simple. The links are stated clearly so that the customers have the idea of which link they should click. The site is not cluttered with unnecessary or unwanted information. There is no misleading information in the site.
